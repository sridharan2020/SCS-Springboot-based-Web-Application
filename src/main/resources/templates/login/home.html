About Us

The Student Counselling Services (SCS), also known as SAKHA, is an Institute level faculty-student volunteer body that endeavors to identify challenges faced by the student community, understand them, create awareness, and help transform the understanding into decisive action.


Our Vision

SCS aims to create an environment of progress and harmony to generate skilled and healthy (both at mentally & physical level) human resources in the institute with adequate potential, who can further contribute to the development of the nation in various areas of science, technology, and humanities.


Our Mission

SCS provides an integrated system of counselling experts, dedicated students, and faculty members to cater to various academic, career, and life needs of the student fraternity. We aim to provide a support system for each individual in college seeking personal or professional growth in life and career.
